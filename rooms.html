Things to know before you search

Short-stay = monthly premium

Turnkey short-stay studios on 33m2 / LiveAnywhere / Wehome cost more per month than a standard 1-year lease. The confirmed listings here run ₩520k–₩950k/mo — that is the realistic floor for fully-furnished, no-ARC, English-contract rooms.

Hoegi Station is the fastest route

Line 1 direct to KHU back gate in 2 minutes on foot (or front gate in 15 min). The confirmed ₩74만 listings are literally at the back gate — hard to beat on price + commute combined.

Line 6 needs a transfer at Seokgye

Any Line 6 neighbourhood routes through Seokgye to reach Line 1 for KHU. Budget 40–55 minutes total. Prices are lower, but transfer time adds up daily.

Keep the deposit small

33m2 deposit = ₩330,000 fixed. No ARC needed. LiveAnywhere uses escrow. These are the safest low-deposit routes for a foreign student arriving without a Korean credit history.

Confirm the full-option checklist

Before booking: washer, fridge, A/C, cooker (gas or induction), desk, bed, and WiFi. Ask whether the maintenance fee (관리비) and utilities are billed separately from quoted rent.

Winter heating (ondol) is expensive

Gas floor heating in January–February can add ₩100k–₩200k/month. Ask who pays gas heating and whether it is included in the quoted weekly price.

Pay through the platform only

Never wire money directly to a landlord before a signed contract. Use the platform's payment flow. Check the property register (등기부등본) and confirm the agent is licensed (공인중개사) for Ziptoss contracts.
